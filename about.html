The (Wo)Man Behind It All

Stressed, blessed, and coffee obsessed (even though I don't drink coffee that often ahahah). Hi! I am Lourdes Angela Rosales of Batch 2026 (Grade 9-Balingkilat). 14 years old, from Tarlac City, Tarlac. This website is coded by yours truly.

At first, I didn't have a name for my website. I thought about it for a while, scrolling through cafe name ideas on google and stuff. Then I suddenly remembered my quarter 1 project on this subject, an online library. I thought, "Hmmm, books and coffee shops go together.. What if I make a cafe version of 'Yours Truly'?" and so I did! And here it is~


Oh! And by the way, thank you for helping me with this by giving your honest opinions and criticisms.. You're the best! I'm touched beyond words... uwu thank you~
